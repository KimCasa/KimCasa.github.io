# Presenting My Research at the UGA REU Symposium

During the University of Georgia Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) in Crop Genetics and Genomics, I had the opportunity to present my research at the end-of-summer symposium.
This project introduced me to plant genomics and computational biology, and it was one of the experiences that inspired me to pursue graduate studies in bioinformatics.
